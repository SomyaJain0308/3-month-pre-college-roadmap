# Weekly Review Log

*Update every Sunday. Takes 15 minutes. Non-negotiable.*

**Why this matters:** Drift is invisible until it compounds. One missed week is fine. Three missed weeks means you're 3 weeks behind with no awareness of it. This log is your early warning system.

## How to Do a Weekly Review (15 min)

1. Open your tracking sheet
2. Answer the 4 questions below honestly
3. Update the phase checkboxes for anything you completed
4. Set your single top priority for next week
5. Done
